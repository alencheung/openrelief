## Getting Started

### Welcome to OpenRelief

OpenRelief is a community-powered emergency coordination platform that connects people who need help with those who can provide assistance during disasters and emergencies. This guide will help you get started quickly and effectively.

### What You Can Do With OpenRelief

- **Report Emergencies**: Alert your community about fires, medical emergencies, natural disasters, and more
- **Receive Alerts**: Get notified about emergencies in your area
- **Verify Reports**: Help confirm or dispute emergency reports to improve accuracy
- **Build Trust**: Earn trust points for accurate reporting and community participation
- **Help Others**: Respond to requests for assistance in your area

### First Steps

1. **Create an account** or sign in as a guest
2. **Enable location services** for accurate emergency reporting
3. **Set your notification preferences** to stay informed
4. **Complete your profile** to build community trust
5. **Take the interactive tutorial** to learn key features

## Account Setup

### Creating Your Account

#### Option 1: Email Registration

1. Click "Sign Up" on the home screen
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Check your email for a verification link
4. Click the verification link to activate your account
5. Complete your profile with basic information

#### Option 2: Social Login

1. Click "Continue with Google/Apple/Facebook"
2. Authenticate with your chosen provider
3. Grant necessary permissions for OpenRelief
4. Complete your profile setup

#### Option 3: Guest Access

1. Click "Continue as Guest" for limited access
2. Note: Guest users have limited functionality
3. Upgrade to full account anytime for full features

### Profile Completion

#### Essential Information

- **Display Name**: How others will see you in the community
- **Location**: General area (not exact address) for emergency relevance
- **Contact Method**: How you prefer to be notified
- **Emergency Skills**: Any relevant skills (first aid, languages, etc.)

#### Privacy Settings

- **Location Sharing**: Choose who can see your location
- **Profile Visibility**: Control who can view your profile
- **Notification Preferences**: Set when and how you receive alerts

### Verification Process

#### Phone Verification (Optional but Recommended)

1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Account Verification
2. Enter your phone number
3. Receive and enter the verification code
4. Verified users receive higher trust scores

#### Email Verification

1. Check your inbox for verification email
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account status will update to "Verified"

#### Step 2: Provide Essential Details

1. **Location**: Confirm or adjust the emergency location
   - Use current GPS location

2. **Title**: Brief, clear description (max 100 characters)
   - Example: "Fire in apartment building"

   - What is happening

1. **Severity Level**: Rate the emergency severity (1-5)

   - Add close-ups for important details

   - Phone number for immediate contact
   - Note if you're at the scene or nearby
   - Indicate if you can provide ongoing updates

#### Step 4: Review and Submit

1. **Review all information** for accuracy
2. **Confirm location** is correct
3. **Check severity level** is appropriate
4. **Add any final details** in the notes field
5. **Submit your report** to the community

## Trust System Education

### Understanding Trust Scores

#### What is Trust Score?

Your trust score represents your reliability and accuracy in reporting emergencies. It helps the community prioritize verified information and identify trustworthy sources.

#### Trust Score Components

- **Base Score**: Starting point for all users (0.1)
- **Report Accuracy**: How accurate your emergency reports are
- **Verification History**: Your track record of confirming/disputing reports
- **Community Participation**: How you engage with the platform
- **Account Verification**: Verified status increases trust score

#### Trust Score Ranges

- **0.0 - 0.2**: New user, limited trust
- **0.2 - 0.4**: Basic trust, reports are considered
- **0.4 - 0.6**: Moderate trust, reports carry more weight
- **0.6 - 0.8**: High trust, reports are prioritized
- **0.8 - 1.0**: Excellent trust, reports are highly valued

### Building Trust

#### Accurate Reporting

- **Report only verified information**
- **Provide detailed, factual descriptions**
- **Include photos when safe and appropriate**
- **Update reports as situations change**

#### Community Participation

- **Verify other users' reports** when you have knowledge
- **Provide helpful feedback** on reports
- **Respond to requests for additional information**
- **Participate in community discussions**

#### Consistency

- **Use the platform regularly** but responsibly
- **Maintain accuracy** over time
- **Follow community guidelines**
- **Help improve the system** with constructive feedback

### Trust Score Impact

#### Report Visibility

- **Higher trust scores** mean your reports are seen by more people
- **Priority notifications** are sent for high-trust user reports
- **Emergency services** may prioritize verified reports
- **Community members** rely more on trusted sources

#### Platform Features

- **Advanced features** unlock at higher trust levels
- **Verification privileges** become available
- **Community moderation** opportunities
- **Enhanced reporting** capabilities

### Maintaining Trust

#### Avoid Trust Reduction

- **Don't submit false or misleading reports**
- **Avoid speculative information**
- **Don't abuse the verification system**
- **Follow community guidelines consistently**

#### Recovery from Mistakes

- **Everyone makes mistakes** - learn from them
- **Trust scores recover** with consistent accurate reporting
- **Provide explanations** for any errors
- **Focus on quality** over quantity of reports

## Mobile App Setup

### Installation

#### iOS Installation

1. **Open Safari** on your iOS device
2. **Navigate to** app.openrelief.org
3. **Tap the Share button** (square with arrow)
4. **Select "Add to Home Screen"**
5. **Name the app** "OpenRelief"
6. **Tap "Add"** to install on your home screen

#### Android Installation

1. **Open Chrome** on your Android device
2. **Navigate to** app.openrelief.org
3. **Tap the menu button** (three dots)
4. **Select "Add to Home screen"**
5. **Name the app** "OpenRelief"
6. **Tap "Add"** to install

### Permissions Setup

#### Location Services

1. **Open Settings** ‚Üí OpenRelief ‚Üí Location
2. **Select "While Using the App"** for basic functionality
3. **Enable "Always"** for critical emergency alerts
4. **Set "Precise Location"** for accurate reporting

#### Notifications

1. **Open Settings** ‚Üí OpenRelief ‚Üí Notifications
2. **Enable "Allow Notifications"**
3. **Select alert types** you want to receive
4. **Choose notification style** (banner, alert, etc.)

#### Camera and Microphone

1. **Open Settings** ‚Üí OpenRelief
2. **Enable Camera** for photo/video evidence
3. **Enable Microphone** for voice reports (optional)
4. **Grant permissions when prompted** during use

### App Configuration

#### Emergency Contacts

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Emergency Contacts
2. **Add family members** and trusted contacts
3. **Include medical information** and allergies
4. **Set up ICE (In Case of Emergency)** contacts

#### Notification Preferences

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Notifications
2. **Choose alert radius** (1-50 miles)
3. **Select emergency types** to monitor
4. **Set quiet hours** to avoid disturbances
5. **Configure priority alerts** for critical emergencies

#### Data Usage

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Data & Storage
2. **Choose Wi-Fi only** for large downloads
3. **Enable data saver** mode if needed
4. **Set offline maps** for your area

### iOS-Specific Features

#### Background Processing

1. **Enable Background App Refresh**
   - Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Background App Refresh
   - Toggle OpenRelief to ON
   - Ensures alerts during emergencies

#### Critical Alerts

1. **Enable Critical Alerts** for iOS 12+
   - Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí OpenRelief
   - Toggle "Critical Alerts" to ON
   - Bypasses Do Not Disturb for life-threatening emergencies

#### Apple Watch Integration

1. **Install on Apple Watch** if available
2. **Enable wrist detection** for quick alerts
3. **Configure haptic feedback** for silent notifications
4. **Set up complications** for quick access

### Android-Specific Features

#### Battery Optimization

   - Settings ‚Üí Battery ‚Üí Battery Optimization
   - Find OpenRelief and select "Don't optimize"

#### Emergency Location Service

   - Settings ‚Üí Location ‚Üí Emergency Location Service

## Offline Functionality

### Understanding Offline Capabilities

OpenRelief is designed to work during network outages and emergencies when connectivity may be limited. The app stores critical information locally and syncs when connectivity is restored.

### Offline Features

#### Emergency Reporting

- **Create reports** without internet connection
- **Store reports locally** until connectivity returns
- **Queue notifications** for automatic sending
- **Save location data** with GPS coordinates

#### Map Access

- **Download offline maps** for your area
- **View cached map tiles** without internet
- **Access saved locations** and points of interest
- **Use compass and GPS** for navigation

#### Emergency Information

- **Access first aid guides** offline
- **View emergency procedures** without internet
- **Read safety instructions** stored locally
- **Access contact information** for emergency services

### Setting Up Offline Access

#### Downloading Offline Maps

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Offline Maps
2. **Select your area** from the map
3. **Choose map quality** (standard vs. high detail)
4. **Download maps** for your region
5. **Set auto-update** for regular map updates

#### Emergency Information Storage

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Offline Content
2. **Select emergency guides** to download
3. **Choose first aid instructions** by language
4. **Download contact information** for local services
5. **Set storage limits** to manage device space

#### Sync Configuration

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Sync & Backup
2. **Enable auto-sync** when connectivity returns
3. **Set sync frequency** (immediate, hourly, daily)
4. **Configure backup** for critical information
5. **Test offline functionality** regularly

### Using OpenRelief Offline

#### Reporting Emergencies Without Internet

1. **Open the app** and tap "Report Emergency"
2. **Fill in all details** as usual
3. **The app will show** "Offline Mode - Report queued"
4. **Reports sync automatically** when you reconnect
5. **Check sync status** in Settings ‚Üí Sync Status

#### Receiving Offline Alerts

1. **Enable Bluetooth and Wi-Fi** for mesh networking
2. **Connect to nearby devices** running OpenRelief
3. **Receive alerts** from other users in range
4. **Share information** within your local network
5. **Help build** a temporary emergency communication network

#### Managing Offline Data

1. **Monitor storage usage** in Settings ‚Üí Storage
2. **Clear cached data** if storage is full
3. **Update offline content** regularly
4. **Test offline features** before emergencies
5. **Share offline capabilities** with community members

### Limitations and Considerations

#### Offline Limitations

- **Real-time updates** are not available offline
- **Verification system** requires connectivity
- **Trust score updates** sync when online
- **Some features** require internet connection

#### Best Practices

- **Test offline functionality** regularly
- **Keep maps updated** for your area
- **Maintain device storage** for offline content
- **Share offline knowledge** with community
- **Have backup plans** for communication

## Privacy and Security

### Your Privacy Matters

OpenRelief is designed with privacy as a core principle. We understand that during emergencies, privacy concerns are especially important. Here's how we protect your information.

### Data Collection and Use

#### Information We Collect

- **Location Data**: Only when you explicitly share it for emergencies
- **Account Information**: Email, name, and basic profile details
- **Emergency Reports**: Information you choose to share with the community
- **Device Information**: Technical details needed to provide the service

#### How We Use Your Data

- **Emergency Coordination**: Connect people who need help with those who can provide it
- **Trust System**: Calculate reliability scores for accurate information
- **Service Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve the platform
- **Safety Features**: Ensure appropriate responses to emergencies

#### Information We Don't Collect

- **Personal conversations** between users
- **Detailed browsing history** outside the app
- **Contact lists** from your device
- **Financial information** or payment details

### Privacy Controls

#### Location Privacy

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Location
2. **Choose sharing level**:
   - **Exact Location**: Precise GPS coordinates
   - **Approximate Location**: General area only
   - **City Level**: City or region only
   - **Disabled**: No location sharing

#### Profile Visibility

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Profile
2. **Control who can see**:
   - **Full Profile**: All profile information visible
   - **Basic Info**: Only name and trust score visible
   - **Anonymous**: No profile information visible
   - **Community Only**: Visible only to verified users

#### Emergency Report Privacy

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Reports
2. **Configure report visibility**:
   - **Public**: Anyone can see your reports
   - **Community**: Only verified users can see reports
   - **Emergency Services**: Only official responders can see reports
   - **Private**: Reports visible to you and emergency services only

### Security Features

#### Account Security

- **Two-Factor Authentication**: Add an extra layer of security
- **Encryption**: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Secure Login**: Industry-standard authentication methods
- **Session Management**: Control active sessions on your devices

#### Report Security

- **Verification System**: Community verification prevents false reports
- **Trust Scoring**: Reliable reports are prioritized
- **Moderation**: Inappropriate content is removed quickly
- **Emergency Protocol**: Verified emergencies get immediate attention

#### Communication Security

- **End-to-End Encryption**: Private messages are encrypted
- **Secure Channels**: All communications use secure protocols
- **Data Minimization**: Only necessary information is shared
- **Regular Audits**: Security is regularly reviewed and updated

### Legal and Compliance

#### Data Protection

- **GDPR Compliance**: Full compliance with EU data protection laws
- **Data Portability**: You can request your data at any time
- **Right to Deletion**: You can delete your account and data
- **Transparency**: Clear information about data use and sharing

#### Emergency Exceptions

- **Life-Saving Priority**: During emergencies, privacy may be limited to save lives
- **Official Requests**: We comply with valid legal requests from authorities
- **Emergency Services**: Critical information may be shared with responders
- **Public Safety**: Community safety takes precedence during crises

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### App Installation Problems

**Issue**: Can't install the app on my device
**Solutions**:
- Check device compatibility (iOS 12+ or Android 8+)
- Ensure sufficient storage space is available
- Update your device's operating system
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Try a different browser for installation

**Issue**: App won't add to home screen
**Solutions**:
- Make sure you're using Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android)
- Check that you've visited the full website URL
- Try refreshing the page and attempting again
- Restart your device and try once more
- Contact support if the issue persists

#### Location and GPS Issues

**Issue**: App can't find my location
**Solutions**:
- Enable location services in device settings
- Check app permissions for location access
- Ensure GPS is enabled on your device
- Try moving to an area with better GPS reception
- Restart the app after enabling location services

**Issue**: Location is inaccurate on the map
**Solutions**:
- Wait a few moments for GPS to stabilize
- Enable "Precise Location" in app permissions
- Check if location services are working in other apps
- Try toggling location services off and on
- Report the issue if it continues

#### Notification Problems

**Issue**: Not receiving emergency alerts
**Solutions**:
- Check notification permissions in device settings
- Enable "Critical Alerts" for iOS users
- Verify notification preferences in the app
- Check if Do Not Disturb is enabled
- Ensure internet connection is available

**Issue**: Too many notifications
**Solutions**:
- Adjust notification radius in app settings
- Customize which emergency types trigger alerts
- Set quiet hours to avoid disturbances
- Filter alerts by severity level
- Use priority notification settings

#### Emergency Reporting Issues

**Issue**: Can't submit an emergency report
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection is available
- Verify all required fields are filled
- Ensure location is selected on the map
- Try refreshing the page and submitting again
- Use offline mode if internet is unavailable

**Issue**: Report submission is very slow
**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection speed
- Try switching from cellular to Wi-Fi
- Reduce photo/video size before uploading
- Close other apps using bandwidth
- Submit report without media if urgent

#### Account and Login Problems

**Issue**: Can't log in to my account
**Solutions**:
- Check that you're using the correct email/password
- Try the "Forgot Password" option
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser or device
- Contact support if login continues to fail

**Issue**: Account verification email not received
**Solutions**:
- Check spam/junk folders in your email
- Wait a few minutes for email delivery
- Request a new verification email
- Add OpenRelief to your email contacts
- Contact support with your email address

#### Performance Issues

**Issue**: App is running very slowly
**Solutions**:
- Close other apps running on your device
- Clear app cache in device settings
- Check for available device storage
- Update to the latest app version
- Restart your device completely

**Issue**: Map is not loading or is very slow
**Solutions**:
- Check internet connection stability
- Try switching between map layers
- Clear map cache in app settings
- Reduce map detail level in settings
- Use offline maps if available

### Emergency-Specific Troubleshooting
